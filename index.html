What Makes Our Coffee Special?

The quality of a good coffee is heavily influenced by the quality of the beans, the roast, and the water, along with the freshness of the beans and how well they have been stored.

It also stimulates the release of dopamine, the brain's “feel good” chemical, and that elevates our mood along with the rest. This effect is what makes coffee mildly addictive and what makes us grouchy when we go decaf.

From any point of view what remains after a coffee is a pure feeling of wellness, the true essence of “the pleasure of coffee”.
